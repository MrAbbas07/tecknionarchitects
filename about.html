About Tecknion Architects

We are a team of passionate architects and designers dedicated to creating innovative and sustainable architectural solutions that transform spaces and inspire communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Tecknion Architects has grown from a small design studio to a leading architectural firm in Hyderabad. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainable design practices.

Over the years, we have completed numerous projects ranging from residential complexes to commercial spaces, each reflecting our dedication to quality and attention to detail.

Our Chief Architects

Rahmath Ali

Co-Founder & Principal Architect

With over 20 years of experience in architectural design, Rahmath brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to Tecknion Architects. He holds a Master's degree in Architecture from the prestigious Indian Institute of Architecture and has led numerous award-winning projects throughout his career.

Rahmath specializes in sustainable design and has been a pioneer in implementing eco-friendly architectural solutions across India. His vision for creating spaces that harmonize with nature while meeting modern needs has been the guiding principle of Tecknion since its inception.

M.Arch, Indian Institute of Architecture
LEED Accredited Professional
Architectural Excellence Award, 2019

Chandrashekhar Reddy

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Chandrashekhar is the creative force behind Tecknion's distinctive design aesthetic. With a background in both architecture and interior design, she brings a holistic approach to every project. Her work has been featured in numerous architectural publications and has earned recognition for its innovative use of space and light.

Before co-founding Tecknion, Chandrashekhar worked with leading architectural firms in Singapore and Dubai, bringing international perspective and expertise to the company. She is passionate about creating spaces that tell stories and evoke emotions while remaining functional and timeless.

B.Arch, National Institute of Design
Certified Interior Designer
Top 10 Designers of the Year, 2018
